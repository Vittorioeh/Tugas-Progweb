About NewJeans World Tour

The NewJeans Concert is a musical experience that blends youthful energy with the captivating charm of their music. Fans will be treated to a passionate performance from the members of NewJeans, who will deliver their hit songs with charisma and flair.

Here's what you can expect at a NewJeans concert:

Energetic and Charismatic Performances: NewJeans members are known for their electrifying stage presence. They'll perform their chart-topping tracks with infectious enthusiasm and charisma, leaving the audience swept up in the vibrant concert atmosphere.

Enticing Setlist: The NewJeans Concert will feature their hits from albums like "New Jeans" and "Hype Boy," including "Attention," "Hype Boy," "Cookie," and "Hurt." Fans might even get a taste of unreleased tracks, offering a sneak peek into their musical evolution.

The NewJeans Concert is a must-attend event for K-Pop fans and anyone who enjoys energetic music. Fans can expect a dazzling performance and a lively concert atmosphere.
